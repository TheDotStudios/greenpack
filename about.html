Our Journey

Every big change begins with a
simple idea

2017
The Foundation

GreenPack was founded with a clear belief - packaging could be functional, visually appealing, and environmentally responsible. This year marked the beginning of India’s vision to build an integrated, paper-based tableware and packaging company, with strong groundwork laid through the right machinery, partners, and a sustainability-first mission.

2018
The First Step

A defining moment arrived on Sharad Purnima, October 24th, 2018, when our first machine was installed - symbolizing a purposeful and promising start. By the end of the year, GreenPack processed its first reel and officially entered the biodegradable packaging space.

2019
Growth & New Horizons

With growing demand and confidence, we expanded our die-cutting capacity and doubled production output. This year also saw the conceptualization of Project HoReCa, a focused initiative designed to serve the evolving needs of QSRs and foodservice brands.

2020
Resilience in Adversity

While the world faced uncertainty, GreenPack chose progress. Despite pandemic challenges, we launched our second production line, showcased at AAHAR 2020, and finalized major expansion plans for tableware manufacturing and the upcoming Ahmedabad facility.

2021
Expanding Our Footprint

Our ripple cup line went live, and the Ahmedabad unit became fully operational. This expansion strengthened our presence in Western India and marked GreenPack’s evolution into a truly national manufacturer.

2022
Streamlining for Scale

This year was dedicated to strengthening our core. We automated systems, refined processes, and focused on building a faster, error-free, and quality-driven operation - ensuring consistent delivery and global-standard compliance.

2023
Reinvention & Customization

To meet rising demand for tailored eco-packaging, our Tableware Project moved to Greater Noida. The shift brought increased flexibility, higher capacity, and better customization support for QSRs and corporate clients.

2024
The Next Leap

2024 marks GreenPack’s most ambitious phase yet, with large-scale expansion in biodegradable tableware. Backed by years of learning and innovation, we are stepping forward to lead India’s sustainable packaging movement - shaping a cleaner future, one cup at a time.
