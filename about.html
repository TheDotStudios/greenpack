2017
The Foundation

The GreenPack story began in 2017, born from a vision to build India’s first integrated paper-based tableware and packaging company. By the end of that year, the groundwork was laid — machinery finalized, partners aligned, and a clear mission set: to replace single-use plastic with planet-friendly alternatives.

2018
The First Step

On Sharad Purnima, October 24th, 2018, our first machine arrived — an auspicious start symbolizing light, purpose, and new beginnings. By December, the first reel was processed, and GreenPack officially entered the biodegradable packaging market.

2019
Growth & New Horizons

Momentum followed. We added new die-cutting capacity, doubled our output, and conceptualized Project HoReCa — our dedicated line for Quick Service Restaurants and foodservice packaging.

2020
Resilience in Adversity

When the world slowed down, we sped up. Despite Covid disruptions, we launched our second production line, showcased at Aahar 2020, and finalized major expansions — including machinery for our Tableware Division and the upcoming Ahmedabad plant.

2021
Expanding Our Footprint

GreenPack’s ripple cup line went live, and our Ahmedabad unit began full-scale operations — strengthening our presence across Western India and cementing our position as a national manufacturer.

2022
Streamlining for Scale

We focused inward — automating systems, tightening processes, and building an error-free, agile operation. The goal: faster delivery, consistent quality, and readiness for global standards.

2023
Reinvention & Customization

Our Tableware Project shifted to Greater Noida, adding capacity and flexibility to meet the growing demand for customized eco-packaging from QSRs and corporate clients.

2024
The Next Leap

2024 marks GreenPack’s most ambitious phase yet — a large-scale expansion in biodegradable tableware. We’re leveraging years of learning, optimization, and innovation to lead India’s sustainable packaging revolution.
This year is more than growth — it’s our commitment to shaping a cleaner, greener future for generations to come.

  • A leading multi-outlet Chinese restaurant chain in India

The client approached GreenPack with a clear vision: to create a customized paper container that aligned perfectly with their food presentation, operational needs, and sustainability goals.

The requirement involved developing entirely new container sizes with precise bottom sealing and top knurling. The complexity was high, as specialized machinery was not readily available, making sample development technically challenging

  • A fast-growing Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) chain

The client’s popular burgers and sandwiches were receiving complaints, not due to taste, but because oil and grease seeped through their existing packaging.

Conventional MG poster paper failed to contain grease, leading to messy handling, poor presentation, and declining customer satisfaction.

Paper cups are a direct touchpoint in the tea-drinking experience. For this cafÈ chain, comfort and safety became an unexpected challenge.

  • A large institutional buyer sourcing paper cups at scale

When dealing with millions of units, even a minor price difference matters. The client questioned a ₹0.15 per cup cost difference.

  • A food brand using paper bowls for chilled products

A customer reported that a paper bowl collapsed within 15 minutes, raising concerns over quality consistency.

The product was designed for hot food, but was now used for chilled, moisture-heavy applications, changing how paper behaved.
