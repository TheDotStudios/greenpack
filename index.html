Helping Our Customers Grow

We empower brands through flexible packaging, reliable supply, and sustainable growth.

From startups to established food brands, we offer low MOQs, timely delivery, and practical design support, helping customers scale confidently while maintaining quality, consistency, and eco-conscious packaging standards across markets.

Paper Cups

Lightweight, insulated, and comfortable to hold, perfect for serving hot and cold beverages in cafes, offices, and foodservice setups, available in single-wall, double-wall, and ripple-wall options.

Paper Containers

Perfect for takeaway and food delivery applications. Ideal for curries, biryanis, desserts, and more, available in multiple size options.

Lunch Boxes

Eco-friendly lunch boxes made from 100% virgin kraft board. Suitable for packaging snacks, starters, and grab-and-go food items.

Burger Boxes

Durable boxes crafted from premium virgin white and kraft board. Secure locking design with ample space for custom branding.

Paper Lids

Leak-resistant paper lids made from PE-coated material. Designed for a snug fit across single and multi-layer paper cups.
