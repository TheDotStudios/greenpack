  • Custom Paper Containers for a Chinese Restaurant Chain
Client

A leading multi-outlet Chinese restaurant chain in India

Introduction

The client approached GreenPack with a clear vision: to create a customized paper container that aligned perfectly with their food presentation, operational needs, and sustainability goals.

The Challenge

The requirement involved developing entirely new container sizes with precise bottom sealing and top knurling. The complexity was high, as specialized machinery was not readily available, making sample development technically challenging.

Our Solution

We invested time in understanding the client’s expectations in detail. Despite multiple failed attempts, our engineering and production teams collaborated closely, testing, refining, and innovating until we achieved near-perfect samples, exceeding the original design expectations.

The Result

GreenPack became the only supplier to deliver actual working samples. The client was impressed by our commitment, effort, and attention to detail, leading to strong confidence and long-term collaboration.

Key Takeaway

True customization requires persistence, collaboration, and a willingness to push technical boundaries to bring a client’s vision to life.
